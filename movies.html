IN THE MOOD FOR LOVE

Romance/Drama

Year:2000

Running Time:1Hour 38Minutes

Hong Kong, 1962. Two lonely neighbors, Chow and Su, suspect their spouses are having an affair. Instead of revenge, they form an unusual bond, re-enacting their spouses' romance. As the lines blur, they grapple with desire, betrayal, and a love they can't quite have.

Director:Kar-Wai Wong Writer:Kar-Wai Wong

Actor:Maggie Cheung Man-yuk Tony Leung Chiu-Wai
